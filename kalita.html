KALITA

Handmade by qualified craftsmen of Tsubame-shi, a well-known district in Niigata prefecture for its metalworking craftsmen and their techniques.

It is designed with Kalita's traditional three-hole structure with a flat bottom allows for deep flavour extraction, perfect for lighter roast profiles.

HISTORY

Kalita was found in 1958 in Nihonbashi, Tokyo. They produce quality drip-based-coffee-making equipments and is well known for their paper filters, drippers, and grinders. Now, Kalita has become a household name amoung coffee enthusiasts.

The flat-bottom design and three-hole structure constricts the flow of water, minimizing any water channeling to extract a full bodied cup.

The Wave filter paper features 20 grooves as it sits flat on the bottom of the dripper. This way the water comes in contact mostly with the coffee, called the Wave Zone, allowing for more balanced flavour extraction.

Easy to use and reliable, the Kalita Wave is perfect for beginners and barista champions alike, being chosen as a popular dripper in the World Brewers Cup Championship.
