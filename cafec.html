CAFEC

The Cafec Flower Dripper captures the eye of any barista with its elegant flower-concaved grooves.

Paired with Cafec's premium Abaca+ paper filter technology, this dripper makes for the ultimate customisable experience based on the roast of your beans.

HISTORY

Founded in 1973, Cafec pioneers in Paper Filter Manufacturing. It was the first company who developed the cone-shaped paper filter in the world. Today, its Abaca+ paper filters continues to innovate leading paper filter technology.

Its elegant flower ribs are designed to maintain consistent air circulation and prevent the paper filter from sticking against the dripper, allowing for a clear and deep flavour extraction.

Made out of Tritan resin, it is heat-resistance and strong enough to withstand accidental drops, making it suitable for outdoor camping uses. Additionally, it does not contain any BPA and meets EU regulation standards for sustainable product development.

"Happiest smile by coffee!", Cafec aims to simplify and make delicious coffee brewing accessible at home with family and friends.
