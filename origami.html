ORIGAMI

Inspired by Japanese paper craft, the Origami is a functional yet aesthetic piece of brewing equipment for the right coffee connoisseur.

It stands out for its flexibility in containing both cone and wave paper filters for those who enjoy experimentation in flavour profiles.

HISTORY

A blend of artistic expression and functionality. The Origami dripper exemplifies the delicate art of coffee brewing, allowing for the precise control of water flow through its unique vertical grooves.

Each dripper is carved with 20 vertical grooves that pass air along to bring out the best flavor of the beans.

Handcrafted from the Mino region of Gifu prefecture in Japan, known for their exceptional ceramic craftmanship.

The dripper's ceramic build also provides high thermal conductivity and heat retention, ensuring even coffee extraction throughout the process.

PROFILE

Taste Notes

Extracts rich, sweeter, fuller coffee than Hario V60. Produces a well-balanced acidity profile which maintained as the coffee cooled.
